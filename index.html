How does our algorithm work?

We use the spectrogram of the seismic information to obtain a graph of seismic energy over the measurement time. Then, we apply different filters to isolate seismic event cases, first applying a low-pass filter. We also use a peak detection algorithm to find energy peaks, and we filter the detected peaks to retain only those of some scientific interest. This way, we managed to make accurate cuts to the graphs and thus making them smaller in size. This accomplishes the task because if implemented in the observer it could only send to earth the important data, which is smaller in size, and this way making communication faster and more reliable.
